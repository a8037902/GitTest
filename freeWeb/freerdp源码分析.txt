freerdp-client rdp通用客户端
wfreerdp-client	和windows有关的客户端

WinMain首先调用属于windows客户端的RdpClientEntry(&clientEntryPoints);
然后调用属于通用客户端的context = freerdp_client_context_new(&clientEntryPoints);

RdpClientEntry函数的两个回掉

pEntryPoints->GlobalInit = wfreerdp_client_global_init;
pEntryPoints->ClientNew = wfreerdp_client_new;


wfreerdp_client_global_init会在freerdp_client_context_new里执行pEntryPoints->GlobalInit();
里面调用
freerdp_channels_global_init();
freerdp_register_addin_provider(freerdp_channels_load_static_addin_entry, 0);


pEntryPoints->ClientNew(instance, context);在函数freerdp_client_common_new中调用
而在函数freerdp_client_context_new中有instance->ContextNew = freerdp_client_common_new;
所以最终会在函数freerdp_context_new中调用IFCALL(instance->ContextNew, instance, instance->context);

在函数wfreerdp_client_new中会调用
wfreerdp_client_global_init();
context->channels = freerdp_channels_new();
